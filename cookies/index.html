Compliance

Visible Legal Marketing Limited is a claims management company regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (Authorisation no. 835293) which generates enquiries and referrals for a panel of solicitors. We work with a panel of solicitors. All of our panel firms are solicitors of England and Wales, authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (www.sra.org.uk). Any solicitor we refer you to is an independent professional from whom you will receive impartial and confidential advice. You are free to choose another solicitor. Visible Legal Marketing Limited is a limited company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 10263322). For complaints please contact complaints@vlmclaims.co.uk. If we cannot resolve your complaint, you may refer it to the Financial Ombudsman Service.
