What are cookies? Cookies are small files stored on your device. Some are essential; others (like analytics) help us improve the site.

Types we use

  • Strictly necessary (always on): required for security and basic functionality. No consent needed.
  • Analytics (optional): help us understand usage so we can improve the site. Set only if you accept.

Specific cookies / storage

  • Google Analytics (GA4): measurement cookies (e.g. _ga variants). Set only after you click “Accept analytics”.
  • Local Storage: vlm_cookie_consent — remembers your choice (accepted/rejected) for up to 12 months.
